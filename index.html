About Moses Olanrewaju Bolarin

I’m Moses Olanrewaju Bolarin, widely recognized as LanBo-Moe—a digital transformation strategist, multimedia educator, and entrepreneur devoted to empowering Africa’s future. Over the past decade, I’ve pioneered innovative initiatives in coding, digital literacy, and multimedia, working with numerous schools and community organizations across Nigeria to unlock the potential of young minds.

My philosophy is rooted in blending creativity with technology and local insights with global perspectives. I believe that when passion meets opportunity, transformation is inevitable. Join me as we inspire, empower, and shape a future where every young African has the chance to excel in a rapidly evolving world.

Because the future belongs to those who dare to dream and innovate.

.

Faith & Family

Saved in 1986 while still in high school, Moses Olanrewaju Bolarin has dedicated his life to ministry and entrepreneurship. Moses Olanrewaju Bolarin is a multifaceted individual whose life and work bridge faith, technology, and creativity. With over three decades of ministry experience, he has impacted countless lives through his teachings, writings, and leadership. He is blessed with two children, Misi and Mayo.Through his writings, music, teachings, and mentorship programs, Moses is committed to leaving a legacy that will continue to impact generations to come.

50th Birthday Milestone

2024 marks Moses' Golden Jubilee celebration, with December 3rd being his 50th birthday. This milestone year includes the special release of "Mysteries of the Kingdom – Book 3," the culmination of his acclaimed trilogy.

Professional Background

A graduate of the University of Lagos with a degree in Computer Science and a Post Graduate Diploma in Public Relations, Moses has established himself as a respected figure in the I.T. and multimedia sectors. He was ranked in the top 6% of Nigerian executives in 2015 and received the prestigious "Award of Excellence."

Entrepreneurial Legacy
